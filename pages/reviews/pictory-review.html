What is Pictory?

Pictory is an innovative AI video platform that transforms text content into professional videos automatically. Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Seattle, WA, the platform has grown to serve over 500,000 users worldwide.

Perfect for content creators, marketers, and businesses who want to repurpose existing blog posts, articles, and scripts into engaging video content without expensive equipment or video editing skills.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Video: Automatically create videos from articles or blog posts
  • Video Summarization: Transform long videos into concise short clips
  • AI Voiceover: Choose from 60+ realistic AI voices in multiple languages
  • Stock Media: Access millions of royalty-free images and video clips
  • Brand Kit: Apply consistent branding across all videos
  • Auto-Caption: Automatically generate and embed captions
