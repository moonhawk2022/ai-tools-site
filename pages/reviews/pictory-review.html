What is Pictory?

Pictory is an AI video platform that automatically transforms text content into professional videos. Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Seattle, the platform has grown to serve over 500,000 users according to their official website.

In 2026, Pictory remains the best tool for content repurposing - turning existing text, blogs, and long videos into publishable short-form content faster than any competitor.

Key Features 2026

  • Blog/URL to Video: Paste any article URL → AI extracts content → auto-generates scenes. This is the most popular workflow according to Trustpilot reviews.
  • Script to Video: Write or paste a script → AI matches stock footage to each segment for professional results.
  • Video Summarization: Upload long videos → AI identifies highlights → creates short clips for social media.
  • Transcript-Based Editing: Edit text directly → video cuts automatically. A unique feature that sets Pictory apart from competitors.
  • AI Voiceovers: 120+ voices via ElevenLabs integration with multilingual support.
  • Auto Captions: 98% accuracy for English, customizable styling for brand consistency.
  • Screen Recorder: Record screen + webcam for creating tutorials directly in the platform.
  • PPT to Video: Convert presentations to video (50-500 slides/month depending on plan).
  • Stock Media: 15-18M stock assets from Shutterstock (Professional+ plans).
  • Brand Kit: Apply consistent branding with logo, colors, intro/outro across all videos.

What Pictory Does NOT Have

  • ❌ AI Avatars - No digital humans or lip-sync avatars (use HeyGen/Synthesia instead)
  • ❌ Video Translation - Limited multilingual capabilities
  • ❌ Generative AI Video - No Sora/Runway-style cinematic generation
  • ❌ Real-Time Collaboration - Only available on Teams+ plans

How to Use Pictory (Step-by-Step)

Workflow 1: Blog Post → Video (Most Popular)

  1. Choose "Article to Video" → Paste URL or paste text directly
  2. AI Analysis — Pictory scrapes content → suggests scene breaks (~2 minutes)
  3. Review Script — Edit text, adjust scene lengths, delete sections
  4. Select Visuals — AI suggests stock footage; swap any scene manually
  5. Add Voiceover — Choose AI voice or upload your own
  6. Auto-Captions — Generate, customize font/color/position
  7. Brand — Apply brand kit (logo, colors, intro/outro)
  8. Export — 720p (Starter) or 1080p (Professional+)

Workflow 2: Long Video → Short Clips

  1. Upload video or paste YouTube URL
  2. AI Highlights — Automatically identifies engaging moments
  3. Select clips — Choose which segments to extract
  4. Auto-resize — 16:9 → 9:16 for Shorts/Reels/TikTok
  5. Add captions — Essential for social media performance
  6. Batch export — Download all clips
