What is Jasper?

Jasper is an AI-powered writing platform that has established itself as one of the leading solutions in the market since its founding in 2021. Headquartered in Austin, Texas, the platform has grown to serve over 100,000 users worldwide.

The platform distinguishes itself through its extensive template library, brand voice customization capabilities, and enterprise-grade features designed for marketing teams and content creators.
