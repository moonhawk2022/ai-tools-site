What is Surfer SEO?

Surfer SEO is a comprehensive SEO platform that helps content creators and marketers produce search-engine-optimized content. Founded in 2017 and headquartered in San Francisco, CA, the platform has grown to serve over 150,000 users worldwide.

The platform combines AI-powered writing assistance with powerful keyword research tools and real-time content optimization, making it a complete solution for content-driven SEO strategies.

Key Features

  • Content Editor: Real-time SEO suggestions as you write with live scoring
  • Keyword Research: Access to 700+ domain metrics for comprehensive keyword analysis
  • SERP Analysis: Understand what's ranking and why with detailed competitor insights
  • Outline Builder: AI-powered content structure generation in seconds
  • Content Audit: Identify issues with existing content and get optimization recommendations
  • Integrations: Works with Google Docs, WordPress, and Jasper AI
